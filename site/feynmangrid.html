FeynmanGrid


We offer a HPC Cluster service which you can use to run your applications, renting CPU time & disk space. We call it FeynmanGrid in the honour of the great physicist Richard Feynman.

FeynmanGrid service is managed & supported entirely by CompecTA, taking advantage of years experience in performance optimizing HPC Clusters & first class support.

Our cluster uses Intel’s best Xeon CPUs with FDR Infiniband interconnects (56Gbit/s) and our modern and flexible file system solution, the FeynmanStor Z.
