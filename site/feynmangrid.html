FeynmanGrid™


Rent CPU Time

We offer an HPC Cluster service which you can use to run your applications, renting CPU time & disk space. We call it FeynmanGrid™ in the honour of the great physicist Richard Feynman.

Dedicated Support

FeynmanGrid™ service is managed & supported entirely by CompecTA, taking advantage of years experience in performance optimizing HPC Clusters & first class support.

Performance Optimized

Our cluster uses Intel’s state-of-the-art Xeon CPUs with FDR Infiniband interconnects (56Gbit/s) and our modern and flexible file system solution, the FeynmanStor Z™.
