Get Started with High Performance Computing

CompecTA’s HPC Starter Pack is the perfect HPC Cluster to get you started with High Performance Computing. We have designed and developed an affordable, efficient and scalable cluster for small teams or individuals, which performs magnitudes of faster than any workstation.

Affordable & Efficient

HPC Starter Pack utilizes HPC best practises with an additional bump from CompecTA’s experience. Currently Starter Pack has two versions, one is built on top of gigabit ethernet interconnects so it’s a lot more affordable. The second version uses 56 Gb/s Infiniband, which significantly accelerates MPI traffic and speeds up any parallel application.

Batteries Included

HPC Starter Pack comes with bundled one year application level support. CompecTA's HPC experts will assist you with applications installations, system administration and also hardware & software problems. All included with the cluster.

Scalable

We would love to see you expand your HPC Starter Pack cluster. For this we do our part and provide you with opportunity to buy back the switches you would have to replace to scale your cluster. This means more affordable scalability for you.
